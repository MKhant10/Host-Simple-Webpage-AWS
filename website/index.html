Htet Arkar Container Transportation

Htet Arkar is a family-owned container transportation service founded in January 2021 in Yangon, Myanmar. We specialize in port-to-warehouse container delivery from Myanmar Industrial Port and Asia World Port Terminal to warehouses across Mingaladon, Shwe Pyi Thar, Hlaing Thar Yar, East Dagon, and South Dagon. With a fleet of five 40-foot semi-trailer trucks, we focus on careful handling, fast turnaround, and dependable service. Our drivers are skilled, polite, and professional, with strong communication to ensure smooth coordination from pickup to delivery.
